## What's inside the kit

**1. tracker.html — the standalone web app**
Single HTML file, ~70KB. Open it in any browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). It just works — no install, no signup, no internet required. Your data stays in your browser via localStorage. Backup and restore as JSON anytime.

**2. Notion template (importable .zip)**
Same 8 sections, fully built out as Notion databases with formulas. Drag the file into Notion, click "Import," done. For people who already live in Notion.

**3. Goal-Setting Guide (PDF)**
A 7-page guide on how to plan your year across all 8 dimensions. Includes the weekly review framework I actually use, plus example goals from my own 2026 plan.

**4. Printable Planner (PDF, A4 + US Letter)**
Quarterly review pages and monthly planner pages, designed to print and stick on a desk if you prefer paper.

## FAQ

**Is my data really private?**
Yes. Open the HTML file, then open your browser's DevTools → Application → Local Storage. You'll see your data sitting there, on your machine, in plaintext. Nothing else. No server, no cloud, no me.

**Will it work on mobile?**
The web app works on mobile browsers but is optimized for desktop. The Notion template works perfectly on Notion mobile.

**What if my browser data clears?**
Use the Export JSON button in Settings to back up. I recommend monthly. The Notion template auto-syncs through Notion's own infrastructure.

**Refunds?**
14-day no-questions refund. Email me, I refund.

**Why is this so cheap?**
Because the free demo is the full app, and I'd rather have 100 happy users than 10 expensive ones. Also, ₹499 / €6.99 is impulse-buy territory — which means more people actually use it instead of regret-purchasing.

**Will there be a v2 with cloud sync?**
Maybe. I'm watching how many of the first 100 buyers ask for it. If demand is there, sync will be optional and self-hosted (BYO Supabase or Postgres) — never a forced subscription.

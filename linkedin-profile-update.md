## 2. About / Summary section

```
I'm a software engineer who builds simple, focused tools for professionals who want to stay on top of their year.

My current project — 2026 Life OS — is a yearly progress tracker I originally built for myself: one dashboard to see whether I'm actually moving on the things that matter — goals, certifications, projects, finances, job search, travel, and ideas. After a few colleagues asked for a copy, I shipped it as a product.

The tracker is local-first (your data stays in your browser, nothing is sent to a server), works offline, and ships as a standalone HTML file plus a Notion template. It's used by professionals in India and across DACH.

Try the free demo: [YOUR DEMO LINK]
Get the full kit: [YOUR GUMROAD LINK]

I'm interested in roles, collaborations, and conversations around:
• Indie product / SaaS engineering
• Frontend / full-stack roles in India or remote-EU
• Productivity tooling, knowledge work, and life-OS-style products

Reach me at [your email] or DM here.
```

**Why this works:**
- Opens with "what I do" not "where I work" — better for personal brand
- Names the product → discoverable when people search "life OS" / "yearly tracker"
- Adds specific geo signal (India, DACH) → DACH recruiters will surface you
- Closes with what you're open to → makes outreach easy
- Both demo and paid links → you stay in control even if recruiters don't reach out

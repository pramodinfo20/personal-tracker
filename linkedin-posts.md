# LinkedIn Launch Posts — 4 Drafts

All posts written in English to work across India + DACH simultaneously. Post times in IST and CET below each draft. Always include the demo link in the first comment, not the body — LinkedIn de-ranks posts with external links in the body.

---

## POST 1 — Teaser (post 1 week before launch)

**Best post time:** Monday or Tuesday. 7:30 IST / 4:00 CET (early morning India + late night Germany — covers both audiences for the next 12 hours).

```
For 4 months I've been tracking everything that matters in my life on a single dashboard.

Goals. Certifications. Side projects. Job applications. Investments. Travel. Ideas.

Eight sections. One screen. Updated weekly.

Three things I learned doing it:

→ I dramatically overestimated what I'd actually start. The "Ideas" tab has 17 entries. Three turned into projects.

→ Tracking certifications in study-hours instead of "%" was the biggest unlock. AWS Solutions Architect went from "ugh, sometime this year" to "32 hours done, 48 to go" — and now I'm actually going to finish it.

→ Seeing all eight life areas at once forced honesty. I was telling myself I was working on "Health & Fitness." The dashboard said 20%. So I started running again.

Next week I'm shipping the tracker as a small product so anyone can use it.

If that sounds useful — drop a 👀 below and I'll DM you the link when it goes live.
```

**Why this works:** Story-led, gives away the insights for free, ends with a soft CTA that captures interested users for launch day.
